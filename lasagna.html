Lasagna Recipe

Linc's Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausage
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • dried basil
  • fennel seeds
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 lb mozerella
  • 3/4 cup parmesan
