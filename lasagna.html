Lasagna Recipe

Linc's Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sausage
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • dried basil
  • fennel seeds
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 lb mozerella
  • 3/4 cup parmesan
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F (190C)
  2. Finely chop onion and garlic. Lightly satuee in pan.
  3. Pan sear sausage and ground beef until brown. Add onion and garlic.
  4. While the meat is cooking weigh on herbs/spices in a bowl.
  5. Mix spice blend with tomato sauce, paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  6. Lightly oil base of pan. Layer cooking pan with lasagna noodles.
  7. Add a layer of meat, sauce, onion/garlic, and cheese. Add more or less depending on your preferred taste!
  8. Repeat until ban is full.
  9. Bake for 45 minutes. Always let lasagna set for 20-30 minutes after baking.
  10. ENJOY!
